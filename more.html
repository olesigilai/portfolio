All said and done, I cleared my second time KCPE exams in 2011 and achieved 347 marks despite my several days of absentism in the name of fee collection which by now sounds funny because it was only Ksh.500 for a whole term but it would take a peasant parent 3 weeks to get at least a half of it.

Gideon received an admission letter from Narok Boys High School. In the letter was a fee structure which to a family that had before not admitted a student to a provision school, sounded to have been hacked somewhere and values meddled....

It did not end here I joined Narok Boys and later dropped in form one term two after a series of demonstrations by students which prompted fee clearance upon reporting back to school, which was tough for me. This made me opt for a home day-mixed secondary school. I later tranfered to a boarding school later in the second term of Form two to better my perfomance where I cleared and attained a Grade of B plain in 2016.
